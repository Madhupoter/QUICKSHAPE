What is Vacuum Casting?

Vacuum casting, also known as urethane casting or polyurethane casting, is a manufacturing process that replicates parts and prototypes using silicone molds and polyurethane resins. It is widely regarded for its ability to produce parts with excellent detail, surface finish, and material properties that closely resemble those of injection-molded components.

Why Choose Quick Shape's Vacuum Casting Services?
  • Exceptional Precision: Vacuum casting offers remarkable detail and surface finish, making it ideal for creating prototypes that closely mimic the final product.
  • Versatile Material Options: Quick Shape provides a wide range of polyurethane resins with varying properties, allowing you to select the material that best suits your project's requirements.
  • Cost-Effective Low-Volume Production: Vacuum casting is perfect for low-volume production runs, providing a cost-effective alternative to injection molding.
  • Rapid Turnaround: We specialize in delivering your projects promptly, meeting tight deadlines without sacrificing quality.
