What is CNC Machining?

CNC (Computer Numerical Control) machining is a manufacturing process that involves the use of computer-controlled machines to remove material from a workpiece to create a precise, finished part. CNC machines are capable of achieving tight tolerances and complex geometries, making them ideal for a wide variety of applications.
